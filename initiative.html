After the great response to the Alumni Talk series, we plan to kick off a new talk series .Due to the current online scenario, most of the 2nd and 3rd-year students are unaware of the research going on in the Institute. We have decided to organize weekly sessions about our professors' ongoing projects. We hope they will be insightful and help students looking to build their careers in research.
